My Trip to Peru

In 1972, I went from my home town in Windom, Minnesota to visit my friend Dave Morfitt in Peru because I was tired of boring jobs and wanted to see some of the world. If I had not taken that trip, it is very unlikely that I would have gone to Brockport, met the love of my life, or had the later adventures and quiet times that brought me much pleasure.

This illustrated narrative documents that trip. If you have any comments, please send me an email at bc.pub19-peru@yahoo.com
