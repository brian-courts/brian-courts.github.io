About those castrations:

Dave and I rode a boat (powered by an outboard motor) to a ranch. I had exaggerated when I wrote in my letter that "he and I" castrated the calves.

Actually, Dave and the rancher immobilized the calf while I stood off to the side. They used a castration process called "banding".

I winced as each band was applied.

With blood supply cut off, the scrotum and testicles atrophy and fall off within 20 to 40 days. Veterinarians say there is some pain initially, but numbness sets in quickly. I wonder how many veterinarians have personally tested that theory.
